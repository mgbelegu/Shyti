About us

Shyti Consulting was established in 2019 by Legal Auditor Loren Shyti and has operated in the field of financial consulting, accounting, and assurance services.

The economic environment, and especially the fiscal one in Albania, are constantly evolving and this brings the need for constant updating to anticipate risks and to realize the most optimal decision-making of Albanian and foreign entrepreneurs. Over 10 years of experience in the financial field, as well as continuous training, assure sufficient experience to provide quick and efficient services, as well as guarantee their quality.

Our ambition is to provide services according to the needs of customers which will serve as a strong and objective basis for their decision-making and success. By combining the basic principles of professional competence and ethics, with a flexible approach to achieving cost-effective services, and with the help of our network of trusted partners, we provide the support that entrepreneurs need for their smooth development.

Services

Audit and Assurance Services

  • Audit of Financial Statements in accordance with National and International Financial Reporting Standards (Legal Audit)
  • Tax Audit of Financial Statements in accordance with applicable law to check compliance with taxes and their calculations
  • Audit / Review of accounting and internal control systems
  • Review of Financial Statements, where auditing is not necessary, to provide limited assurance about their compliance with applicable regulatory framework
  • Various assurance services requiring independent evaluation of a particular financial information or process

Accounting and Declaration Services

  • Establishment of the systems of the Department of Finance and Accounting as well as their maintenance
  • Compilation of Financial Statements according to National or International Financial Reporting Standards
  • Bookkeeping
  • Statements in e-Filing, e-Albania (Payroll, Books for sale-purchase, VAT, Income Tax, Financial Statements)

Consulting Services

  • Financial Consulting
  • Fiscal Consultancy
  • Management consulting and accounting
  • Establish functional reporting and internal control systems
  • Consultancy for actions with the central tax administration (VAT Refund, Tax Representative, Double Tax Avoidance Agreement, etc., Interpretations)
  • Consulting for new ventures (start-up)

Evaluation Services

  • Estimation of payroll calculations
  • Economic valuations, interest, late interest
  • Valuation or revaluation of different items of financial statements
